Cribbage Hand Scanner
Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 2026
Overview

Cribbage Hand Scanner is a mobile application that uses your device's camera to photograph playing cards and calculate your cribbage hand score. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how the app works.

The short version: we don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. We don't require you to create an account. We don't know who you are.

Camera & Images

Cribbage Hand Scanner uses your device camera solely to photograph your playing cards. When you take a photo:

The image is temporarily sent to a secure processing server to identify the cards using AI vision technology. The image is used only to detect the card ranks and suits and is not stored, logged, or retained after processing is complete.

We do not build any database of your photos. We do not use your images for training AI models. Images are discarded immediately after card detection is performed.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any of the following:

Personal information — no name, email address, phone number, or account information is required or collected.

Location data — the app does not access or track your location.

Device identifiers — we do not collect device IDs, advertising IDs, or any other device-specific identifiers.

Usage analytics — we do not track how you use the app, how many hands you score, or any behavioral data.

Advertising

Cribbage Hand Scanner displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google's advertising SDK may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy, which may include device information and advertising identifiers for the purpose of serving relevant ads.

We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

OpenAI (GPT-4o) — used to process card images and identify playing cards. Images are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this processing.

Google AdMob — used to display banner advertisements within the app.

We do not share any personal data with these services because we do not collect any personal data to share.

Children's Privacy

Cribbage Hand Scanner is rated for Everyone and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that require personal data from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
